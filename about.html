Our Story

Lachchhiwala’s began with a simple idea — to revive the authentic taste of home through handcrafted seasoning salts and flavourful blends inspired by Indian culinary tradition.

Rooted in family recipes and generations of craftsmanship, every Lachchhiwala’s creation blends nostalgia with purity — delivering perfectly balanced flavours that feel both familiar and timeless.

Founder & CEO

Guided by passion, patience, and precision, our Founder & CEO envisioned a brand that celebrates authentic seasoning and mindful cooking, where every pinch enhances the soul of a dish.

With a clear vision and deep respect for tradition, the CEO ensures that every Lachchhiwala’s blend reflects consistency, purity, and the joy of sharing great food.

CTO – Quality & Process Innovation

Our CTO bridges tradition with modern food craftsmanship — focusing on process excellence, quality control, and flavour consistency.

By refining blending techniques and maintaining strict standards, the CTO ensures that every batch delivers the same rich taste, purity, and balance.

Craftsmanship

Every Lachchhiwala’s seasoning salt is handcrafted in small batches with love and attention to detail by skilled artisans.

From sourcing the finest natural salts and spices to preserving traditional blending techniques, our process honours quality, purity, and balance — ensuring that every sprinkle brings depth, warmth, and unforgettable flavour.
